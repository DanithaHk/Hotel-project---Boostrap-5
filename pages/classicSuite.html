CLASSIC SUITE

With three of the four Classic Suites featuring their own private plunge pools, these elegant 670 sq.ft (62 sq.m) abodes enable effortless tropical living. Claiming magnificent ocean or tranquil garden views, each opens onto an expansive private terrace with twin daybeds, while inside curvilinear wood and brass rod Lanka chairs are offset by crisp white linen and cozy details, ensuring the perfect retreat for private time out. Sharing a roofline, two Classic Suites can be interconnected, while the other two can be paired with two of the Family Suites.

Amenities

  • Private plunge pool in three of the four suites
  • Private terrace overlooking the Indian Ocean or resort gardens with two daybeds
  • King-size bed
  • Bathroom scales
  • Open plan sitting area with desk
  • Large bathroom with twin basins, free-standing bath, separate rain shower, and WC. Some with outdoor rain shower
  • Two suites connect with two Family Suites via a shared entrance, and two suites interconnect with each other via a corridor
